Catch The Basketball        

game rules:

1. Catch the blue basketball with the blue basket score +10.

2. Catch the red basketball with the red basket score +10.

3. Catch the blue basketball with the red basket score -5.

4. Catch the red basketball with the blue basket score -5.

5. Game is over when you miss 5 basketballs.

key pressings:

1. Press 'A' to move the red basket left.

2. Press 'D' to move the red basket right.

3. Press Keyleft to move the blue basket left.

4. Press KeyRight to move the red basket right.
